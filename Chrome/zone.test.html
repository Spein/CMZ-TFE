FAQ

What is CoMonz Extension?

Comonz is a free browser extension that will allow you to easily fund any type of content or creator. Once added to the browser, you will have the opportunity to create a profile and fill a wallet to the amount completely free. You just have to enjoy surfing with the same habits as always. Each creator for whom you express your interest will receive an equivalent portion of your wallet. Thus, for a Hubous loaded at 100 CoMonz and for 10 content of creators visited monthly, you would offer 10 CoMonZ to each
